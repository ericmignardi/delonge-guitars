Starcaster

Fender

Tom DeLonge, known for his signature sound with Blink-182, has recently made a notable switch to using a Fender Starcaster guitar. Known for its unique offset body and wide tonal range, the Starcaster offers Tom a fresh sonic palette, blending his familiar punk roots with more expansive, atmospheric textures.

ES-333

Gibson

Tom DeLonge has also been associated with the Gibson ES-333, a semi-hollowbody guitar that combines vintage warmth with modern playability. With its rich, resonant tone and versatile design, the ES-333 became one of Tom's go-to instruments, offering a smooth, balanced sound that complemented his emotive playing style.

Stratocaster

Fender

Tom DeLonge is widely recognized for his use of Fender Stratocasters, particularly in his early Blink-182 days. Known for their sharp, cutting tones and comfortable playability, the Stratocasters helped shape Tom's signature sound, from fast-paced punk riffs to soaring lead lines.
