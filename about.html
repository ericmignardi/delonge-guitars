About

As a lifelong fan of Tom DeLonge’s music, I’ve always been captivated by the guitars that have shaped his sound—from the punk energy of Blink-182 to the atmospheric layers of Angels & Airwaves. This website is dedicated to showcasing those instruments, each one playing a pivotal role in his musical evolution.

From the signature Fender Stratocasters that defined the raw, punchy tone of Blink-182’s anthems in the 90s and 2000s, to the Gibson ES-333 that became central to his work with Angels & Airwaves in the 2010s, Tom’s choice of guitars has been as influential as his songwriting.

This site is a celebration of the guitars that helped create the distinctive soundscape we know and love. Whether you’re a fan of Blink, Angels & Airwaves, or Tom’s overall journey, I hope this collection gives you a deeper look at the instruments that have defined his career.
